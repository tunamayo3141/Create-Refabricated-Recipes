# Create: Refabricated Recipes

A Fabric mod that adds useful, balanced, and scientifically plausible recipes for **Create Fly** (the modern Fabric port of Create).

## About
This mod adds 23 new recipes for **Create Fly**, including crushing, compacting, bulk haunting, and mixing recipes. Most recipes are based on scientifically plausible processes and can be individually toggled on/off via a config file.

This is my first mod! And I am no CS major or anything like that, just enough knowledge to fall to the rabbit hole of Minecraft modding. I will go out of my way to tell that most of the Java code was from various AI bots. I really do not know a thing about Java, and Minecraft modding in extension. I only know Python and JSON from a basic CS course and that's it. Anyway, feedback and suggestions are of course welcome (as long as I am not lazy enough haha).
## Why another recipe mod?
Because I want to.

Jokes aside, **Create Fly** does not have much addons to it (at all), so I went out of my cave and made one. Initially made for myself only as a datapack, since I enjoyed playing Create with addons on NeoForge. I wanted to automate everything in my world, and vanilla Create offers little for those who like hoarding stuff late game.

The creation of the recipes are heavily inspired by **Create: Ultimate Factory** and my knowledge on various scientific fields. I'm a nerd, yes.
## Features

- Adds twenty-three (23) balanced and sensible recipes to enable automation of almost everything late game (post-Brass Age) with resource hoarders and builders in mind
- Some recipes closely drew inspiration from real geology, chemistry, and physics
- Toggleable recipes via a (very basic) JSON config file
- Currently only available on Fabric 26.1.2
- I might tweak some recipes in the future, or even add a custom block/item to expand my catalyst idea (depends if I survive fabric modding 101 and if I have the time)

Recipes can be viewed via **JEI** and its siblings.

## Recipes
**1. Stone Generators**
- Adds three (3) stone generators through heated compacting recipes to allow high throughput farms for late game without sacrificing FPS to drills and minecart/stationary contraption block breakers
- Only available post-Brass Age and netherite to not break the game balance
- Inspired from seed catalysts use in nanomaterials and industrial chemistry processes

**2. Renewable Gunpowder**
- Drawn from the recipe for black powder, using a heated mixing recipe
- Cinder flour acts as the source of potassium nitrate (saltpeter) and sulfur. Charcoal is charcoal, and sugar is there for extra oomph (I know because I tried ;>).
- Chucks out 80% of input items to simulate impurities
