# Changelog - Create: Refabricated Recipes

All notable changes to this project will be documented in this file.

The format is based on [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/en/1.0.0/),
and this project adheres to [Semantic Versioning](https://semver.org/spec/v2.0.0.html).

## [1.0.0] - 2026-06-29

### Added
- Initial release of Create: Refabricated Recipes
- 23 toggleable recipes for Create Fly

### Recipes Added

#### Crushing
- Netherite Scrap from crushing Nether Bricks (0.3% chance)
- Wither Skeleton Skull from crushing Scorchia (0.1% chance)

#### Compacting
- Cobbled Deepslate from superheated compacting of Cobblestone catalyzed by Powdered Obsidian
- Diamond from superheated compacting of Coal Blocks
- Rose Quartz from superheated compacting of Quartz and Redstone
- Tuff from compacting Cobblestone, Flint, and Lava
- Basalt Generator from heated compacting of Water and Lava catalyzed by Blue Ice and Netherite Scrap
- Cobblestone Generator from heated compacting of Water and Lava catalyzed by Powdered Obsidian and Netherite Scrap
- Limestone Generator from heated compacting of Honey and Lava catalyzed by Powdered Obsidian

#### Bulk Haunting
- Coal from haunting Charcoal
- Netherrack from haunting Basalt
- Wither Rose from haunting Rose Bushes (40% chance)

#### Mixing
- Asurine from mixing Cobblestone, Water, and Zinc Nuggets
- Calcite from mixing Limestone and Water
- Crimsite from mixing Cobblestone, Water, and Iron Nuggets
- Glowstone Dust from heated mixing of Prismarine Crystals, Glow Berries, and Lava
- Gunpowder from heated mixing of Charcoal, Cinder Flour, Sugar, and Water
- Lapis Lazuli from heated mixing of Iron Nuggets, Calcite, and Cinder Flour
- Leather from heated mixing of Rotten Flesh, Calcite, and Water
- Ochrum from mixing Cobblestone, Water, and Gold Nuggets
- Polished Rose Quartz from mixing Rose Quartz, Sand, Gravel, Powdered Obsidian, and Water
- Redstone from mixing Cinder Flour, Iron Nugget, and Water
- Veridium from mixing Cobblestone, Water, and Copper Nuggets

### Technical Details
- Fabric API: 0.153.0+26.1.2
- Minecraft: 26.1.2
- Requires: Create Fly, Fabric API
- Server-side compatible
- Uses custom `ResourceCondition` for recipe toggles

### Known Issues
- None reported yet

### Notes
- This is my first mod! Feedback is welcome
- Recipes are mostly based on scientifically plausible processes
- All recipes can be toggled via config
